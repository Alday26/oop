Beginner

Simple bodyweight exercises like squats and push-ups, done 3-4 times a week to build basic strength and endurance. Ideal for those just starting out with home workouts.

Intermediate

Add weights and more intense exercises like burpees and dumbbell squats, 4-5 times a week. This level challenges the body to increase strength and stamina.

Advance

High-intensity workouts (HIIT) and heavier weights, done 5-6 times a week. Perfect for those looking for a serious challenge and muscle building.
